Introduction

Our brand identity is made up of a number of elements. The logo, the typography, the color palette, the imagery, and the words. Each element has its own role to play. Together these elements create Brushfire.

Logo Specifics

Primary Logo

Our logotype must always be 1/10th the width of the Brushfire logomark away and centered vertically.

Clearspace

To preserve the integrity of the logotype no other logos or graphics can infringe on its space. The minimum clearspace is half the width

Colors

Typography

Montserrat and Lato as our two primary application typefaces. Mrs Eaves XL is used for marketing and external facing assets. Montserrat is to be used for headlines, subheads and buttons. Lato is to be used for in application body copy.

Mrs Eaves XL is to be used for body copy, quotes, subtitles on marketing material.

For web and social icons we use FontAwesome v4.
